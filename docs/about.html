About the Protocol

The Beginning

From Broken, Burned Out, and 50 Pounds Overweight…

To Building the System That Changes Lives

A few years ago, the founder was standing next to a crashed motorbike — a bruised and broken body bleeding from multiple wounds after being hit by a car while riding a bike.

As a former strength athlete who had spent years building physical capability, suddenly having to relearn how to walk wasn't in the plan. And it happened twice in the same year.

Two crashes. Chronic pain. Inflammation that couldn't be explained. Stress levels that made sleep impossible.

The Breaking Point

The Physical Toll

Two major accidents. Chronic pain. Inflammation that couldn't be explained. And stress levels that made sleep feel impossible. Running on caffeine, deadlines, and damage control.

How It Looked From Outside

On paper, life looked good. Business was moving forward. There were clients to coach, a career to build, a life to maintain.

But the body was falling apart.

The Inside Reality

Joints ached. Eating habits became inconsistent. Energy levels crashed. Belly kept growing. And the belief started creeping in: "This is just how it is after 40."

That was the lie. And it's time to stop believing it.

How the Solution Was Built

No starvation. No 6-day-a-week gym commitments. No "full monk mode" living off broccoli and discipline.

Instead, a system was built that changed everything:

  • ✓ Training just 2–3× per week — minimal time, maximum impact
  • ✓ Hormone and sleep optimization — naturally, without medication
  • ✓ Fat loss while still eating favourite foods
  • ✓ Habits so simple they could be followed during the busiest, most stressful periods

The Result?

50 pounds lost. Stronger, clearer, and leaner than a decade prior — and turned into a system that now works for dozens of high-achieving men just like the clients served today.
