SMS Messaging Terms & Compliance

1. SMS Program Description

Demi Cycle Protocol LLC operates an SMS messaging program to communicate with prospective and current coaching clients. By opting in, you agree to receive text messages related to:

  • Health and fitness coaching program information
  • Follow-up communications after lead form submission
  • Appointment scheduling and reminders
  • Promotional offers related to our coaching services
  • Program updates and relevant fitness-related content

This messaging program sends appointment scheduling and reminder messages to individuals who have opted in via Demi Cycle Protocol LLC's Meta (Facebook/Instagram) lead forms or via our scheduling forms at https://agostonbabicz.github.io/dcp-website, and have explicitly opted in to receive SMS notifications. Opt-in is collected via web forms with a dedicated checkbox for SMS consent.

2. How to Opt In

You opt in to receive SMS messages from Demi Cycle Protocol LLC by submitting your phone number through our Facebook Lead Ad form or website form. By providing your phone number and checking the SMS consent checkbox, you expressly consent to receive text messages from us.

3. Cancellation Instructions

You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Simply text "STOP" to the same number that sent you messages. Upon sending "STOP," we will confirm your unsubscribe status via SMS. Following this confirmation, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us. To rejoin, sign up as you did initially, and we will resume sending SMS messages to you.
