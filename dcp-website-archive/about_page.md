## The Story

A few years ago, I was standing next to a crashed motorbike and a bruised & broken body bleeding from a hundred wounds.

As a former powerlifting champion easily benching 335 lbs, getting hit by a car while riding my bike and having to learn to walk again wasn't on my bingo card for life.

**And it happened twice within the same year — 2021.**

- Two crashes
- Chronic pain
- Inflammation I couldn't explain
- Stress levels that made sleep feel impossible

I was running on caffeine, deadlines, and damage control.

On paper, life looked good — I was studying, coaching my first few clients, recovering, and I even sang in a couple rock bands.

**But my body felt like it was falling apart.**

- My joints ached
- My eating habits became inconsistent
- My belly kept growing
- My energy was shot

And the worst part?

I started believing this was just how men over 40 were supposed to feel — and one day this would be my new "normal" too.

**That was the lie. And I'm glad I stopped believing it.**

---

## How I Built the Protocol

I didn't fix things by starving myself.

I didn't start doing CrossFit six days a week.

I didn't go full monk mode and live off broccoli and shame.

**Instead, I built what became the Demi Cycle Protocol:**

- I trained just 2–3×/week
- I optimized my hormones and sleep, naturally
- I lost fat while still eating my favourite foods
- I made my habits so simple that I could follow them even during the busiest and most stressful periods

**The result?**

I lost 50 pounds, got stronger, clearer, and leaner than I'd been in a decade — and turned it into a system that now works for dozens of high-achieving men just like you.
